Cold Pressed Oil
Woowin exports premium-quality cold-pressed oils, extracted using traditional methods to retain their natural nutrients, rich aroma, and authentic flavor. Rice
At Woowin, we specialize in exporting premium-quality Basmati and Non-Basmati rice, sourced from the finest farms to ensure superior taste, aroma, and texture. Our Basmati rice is known for its long, slender grains, rich fragrance, and fluffy texture, making it the perfect choice for biryanis and gourmet dishes. Meanwhile, our Non-Basmati rice varieties, including Sona Masoori, IR64, and Ponni, are ideal for daily consumption, offering a balance of nutrition and flavor. Processed and packaged under stringent quality standards, our rice is free from chemicals and additives, ensuring purity and freshness. Whether for households, restaurants, or large-scale food industries, our rice meets global export standards, delivering authenticity and excellence in every grain. Choose Woowin for rice that embodies tradition, quality, and trust.

Fenugreek Leaf
Woowin exports premium-quality dry fenugreek leaves, known for their distinctive aroma and slightly bitter yet flavorful taste. Commonly used in culinary applications, these leaves enhance the taste of curries, sauces, and bread while also offering numerous health benefits. Rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and essential nutrients, they are widely valued for their digestive and medicinal properties. Our dry fenugreek leaves are carefully sourced, naturally dried to retain their potency, and processed under stringent quality standards to ensure purity and freshness. With customizable packaging and bulk export options, Woowin brings the authentic essence of this versatile herb to global markets.
